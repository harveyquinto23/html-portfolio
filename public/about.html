About Me

Hello, I'm Franz Harvey Quinto, an IT professional and educator passionate about technology and continuous learning. I hail from the scenic province of Nueva Ecija, Philippines, and hold a bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, with a specialization in Network Administration.

Since embarking on my teaching career in 2019, I've had the privilege of sharing knowledge across diverse disciplines. I spent five fulfilling years at a private higher education institution in Nueva Ecija, where I primarily taught ICT courses to maritime students, IT courses to Information Systems students, and ICT to senior high school learners. Additionally, I served as a Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) certification trainer, helping students achieve globally recognized credentials.

In August 2024, I joined PHINMA-Araullo University in Cabanatuan City, where I currently teach Information Technology students. While I deeply enjoy shaping young minds and preparing them for IT careers, my passion for learning has led me to revisit an area of technology that I left unexplored during my college years: web development.

Back in college, I chose to specialize in network administration over web development. However, as the tech landscape evolves, so does my curiosity. To pursue this interest, I recently enrolled in a full-stack web development course on Udemy. This self-paced journey is helping me explore the intricacies of web development, from front-end design to back-end integration. My goal is to transition into a career as a web developer, creating dynamic and user-friendly websites and applications that solve real-world problems.

Beyond teaching and studying, I value self-improvement and growth. I strive to maintain a balance between mentoring others and learning new skills to stay relevant in the ever-changing tech industry.

I’m excited about the future and look forward to the opportunities that lie ahead in both education and web development. Whether I’m in a classroom or coding a new project, I’m driven by a single purpose: to inspire innovation and empower others through technology.
